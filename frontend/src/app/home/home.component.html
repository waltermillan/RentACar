What is the Car Rental System?

This system allows you to manage vehicle rentals efficiently, securely, and quickly. With our platform, you can check all active rentals, add new cars to our fleet, and register new customers easily.

Main Features

Rental Consultation

You can view all active rentals and check the status of each one. Have all the information you need, from vehicle details to return dates, at your fingertips.

Add New Cars

Adding cars to your rental fleet is easy. Simply enter the car details (make, model, year, etc.), and the system will make it available for rental.

Add New Customers

You can register new customers and associate them with rentals. Keep a complete record of all customers, their information, and their rental history.
